# Highlights

- Long-term extreme low-fat diet may be a synergistic risk factor for gastric diseases in Japan.
- Inadequate dietary fat impairs mucosal barrier via phospholipid synthesis, PGE2, and gut microbiota.
- Japanese cohort data show inverse association between fat intake and gastric cancer risk (e.g. JACC Study HR 0.66).
- Dietary fat optimization (20–30% energy) may complement existing gastric cancer prevention strategies.
